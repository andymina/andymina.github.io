Who are we?

Founded by Andy Mina, avid NASA enthusiast, in 2014, Explore NASA aims to have every child across the country interested in space exploration help push the final frontier. Want to know more about Andy? Check out his website here.

Join the Mission

We believe that NASA has something for all students and we're here to help you find it. As a partner program with NASA, our goal is to help students discover space in the ways that interest them. Whether you like robotics, medicine, or graphic design, there's a community here for you. Interested in programming at NASA? Take our coding challenge below.

College students of all majors are welcome to join Explore NASA's summer internship program: Space Rangers. High school students are encouraged to our introductory summer internship: Space Cadets. Check out testimonials from interns placed through Explore NASA.
